Apparently we have to make an essay regarding an African-American biologist. Picked George Washington Carver

Did you know that you could use peanuts to make SHAMPOO? Yes, I'm being entirely real here, you can use things like peanuts to make a lot of things like shampoo, oil, and even soap! This was all discovered by George Washington Carver, an African-American agricultural scientist, using makeshift tools1 and spare materials! Now imagine something with me: If he was able to discover things like peanut soap and shampoo using makeshift tools, then what do you think he could do with more modern science equipment? In my opinion, if he were to get modern scientific tools, he would have been able to discover much more than what he had discovered using just makeshift tools and scrap materials, being able to revolutionize agricultural sciences even more than what we could think of!
